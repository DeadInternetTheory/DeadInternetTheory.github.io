About the non-for-profit

Our non-for-profit group aims to get our world leaders and officials to strive to control artificial intelligience and bots in the internet. We hope to get more restrictive measures and policies on our beloved websites and social medias to make sure that the content we read, react, and interact with are authentic, human-made content and real communication. When you donate to us we will use your funds to help our effort in getting our message heard by the big organisations who run the websites, and the nations which social media is hosted upon.
