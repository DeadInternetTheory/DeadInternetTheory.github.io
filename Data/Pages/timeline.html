The internet, a worldwide system of interconnected computer networks and electronic devices that communicate with each other using an established set of protocols, was first invented on the 1st of January, 1983, and became publicly available on the 30th of April, 1993. This accomplishment revolutionised the modern world, allowing for instant worldwide communication and interaction.

By 1988, the first Internet Relay Chat (IRC) bots were launched. These allowed for various functions to be automated such as managing user lists, performing searches, and providing services like weather updates and game scores.
