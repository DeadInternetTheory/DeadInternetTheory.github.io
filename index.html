The Dead Internet

The Dead Internet Theory

The dead Internet theory is a theory that asserts that the Internet now consists mainly of AI bot activity and automatically generated content manipulated by algorithmic curation.

How to stop it

We can stop the internet from being overrun with AI chatbots, commenters and posters, by convincing our platforms and our governments to create regulations and policies to prevent. Help us convince our world leaders to control the growing use of AI for human interaction and stop this internet robot epidemic.

Why should we stop this

The internet was originally created to connect humans all around the world, it is a great way to share information and media between millions of individuals world wide. When this is replaced with AI generated content, not only are we not actually interacting with humans, but we also run the risk of a fake information pandemic.
